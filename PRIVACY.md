Privacy Policy  
----------------

totp.codes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All codes are generated locally in your browser for maximum security.

### Cookies and Analytics
We do not use cookies or third-party trackers. Analytics data is collected upon entry to the site, including your country, number of visits and device type. This data is solely used for general traffic monitoring and performance insights. We do not sell or share this data with any third parties.

### Security
Your secret key and TOTP codes are processed locally in your browser for maximum security.

### Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 18.

### Changes to this Policy
We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site implies your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
